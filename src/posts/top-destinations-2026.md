# Top 5 Destinations to Visit in 2026

The world is opening up, and 2026 promises to be a year of incredible travel experiences. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, here are our top picks for where to go this year.

## 1. Greece: The Timeless Classic
Greece continues to enchant travelers with its stunning islands, ancient ruins, and delicious cuisine. In 2026, explore beyond Santorini and Mykonos. Try **Naxos** for amazing food or **Milos** for lunar landscapes.

## 2. Japan: Tradition Meets Future
With the Expo 2025 excitement settling, 2026 is the perfect time to visit Japan. Experience the cherry blossoms in spring, the neon lights of Tokyo, and the serene temples of Kyoto.

## 3. Portugal: Europe's Coolest Corner
Portugal remains a top choice for its affordability and charm. Lisbon and Porto are vibrant hubs, but don't miss the golden cliffs of the Algarve and the vineyards of the Douro Valley.

## 4. Italy: The Art of Living
From the canals of Venice to the rolling hills of Tuscany, Italy is always a good idea. 2026 sees new luxury openings in Rome and restored historical sites in Pompeii.

## 5. New Zealand: Adventure Awaits
For those willing to travel further, New Zealand offers unmatched natural beauty. Hike the Southern Alps, visit Hobbiton, and experience the warm Maori culture.
